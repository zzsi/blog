Replaces OLMo 3's sliding-window attention layers with GDN. Full attention layers kept at every 4th position.

Design Decisions (Ablations)

  • GDN over Mamba-2: Mamba-2 performed worse at every scale, even in hybrid configs
  • Interleaved over clustered: distributing attention uniformly lets every part of the network access global context
  • 3:1 ratio over 1:1 or 7:1: best trade-off; all hybrid ratios beat pure transformer at scale
  • Parameter budget: GDN layers have more params per head than attention, so they reduced heads from 32→30 and shrunk head dims to keep total at ~7B and match OLMo 3 throughput
