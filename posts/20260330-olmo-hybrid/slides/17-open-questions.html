Open Questions

What other formula tweaks feel natural?

The current GDN recurrence:

$S_t = \alpha_t S_{t-1}(I - 2\beta_t k_t k_t^\top) + v_t k_t^\top$

Small changes have had outsized impact (neg eigenvalues, decoupled write). What's the next tweak? Per-channel gating (Kimi)? Higher-rank updates? Different decay structures?

Does hybrid help downstream SFT/RL?

OLMo reused OLMo 3's post-training recipe with no tuning. Are hybrid models more fine-tunable? Does the recurrent state give RL signals a different optimization landscape?

Optimal ratio at 70B+ scale

3:1 works at 7B. Kimi Linear also uses 3:1, Nemotron-H uses ~12:1. Does the optimal GDN-to-attention ratio shift as you scale up?

Which linear RNN wins long-term?

GDN+neg eigenvalues, Kimi Delta Attention, and Mamba-3 are all competitive. Active research area. The winner may not exist yet.

Kernel & hardware maturity

fla is functional but less optimized than FlashAttention. Custom hardware (Groq-style) or future CUDA kernels could widen the gap further.
