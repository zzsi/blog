What We Will Cover

Agenda

  • OLMo paper overview
  • The architecture: how GDN + attention layers are combined
  • History: from delta rule to gated delta net
  • The broader landscape: multiple teams converging on hybrid architectures
  • Scaling laws and the theory of why expressivity improves efficiency
  • Practical implications: what changes in a training stack

What you will take away

Frontier LLM architectures are moving beyond pure transformers. Multiple teams (Allen AI, NVIDIA, Alibaba, Moonshot) have independently shown real gains by mixing recurrent layers with attention.

This talk covers what these hybrid models are, why they work, and what the math behind them looks like.
